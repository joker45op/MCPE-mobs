Welcome to MCPE-Mobs Wiki.

Here is the listing of some of the MOBS you can see in Minecraft game.

Creeper

A creeper is a common hostile mob that silently approaches players and explodes. Due to their distinctive appearance and high potential for killing unwary players.

Endermen

Endermen are tall neutral mobs found in all three dimensions, which have the ability to teleport and pick up blocks.

Zombie

Zombies are common undead hostile mobs that deal melee damage and attack in groups.
